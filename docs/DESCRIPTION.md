> Handycam adds subtle, physics-inspired camera movement that makes Minecraft feel like it's being filmed with a real handheld camera.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/OwKWpUORzGI" title="YouTube gameplay preview" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen></iframe>

## Effects

- Walk bob
- Sprint sway
- Strafe tilt
- Forward tilt
- Mouse lead
- Idle shake
- Breath
- Damage shake
- Hit impact
- Bow shot recoil
- Jump shake
- Landing impact
- Crouch shake
- Eat sway


## Configuration

Every effect has its own intensity slider and can be toggled off independently. Open settings through **ModMenu** (Fabric) or the in-game mod menu (NeoForge).


## Dependencies

- [Cloth Config](https://modrinth.com/mod/cloth-config)
- [ModMenu](https://modrinth.com/mod/modmenu) *(Fabric only)*


*Client-side only. Safe to use on any server.*
